How to Play

🚀 Deposit ---

Upon depositing ---, you will receive B4NK. These B4NK are "shares" of the Hourglass - while you hold them in your wallet, they represent your proportionate share of all future Deposit and Withdrawal fees of the contract.

♻ Compound Earnings

When you hold B4NK, you earn --- dividends - Quickly increase your portion of the Hourglass supply by compounding them! This saves an extra transaction of having to withdraw then re-deposit!

💸 Withdraw ---

If you so desire, you can sell your B4NK and collect the underlying --- from the contract. Just enter an amount, press "Withdraw" and check your dividends total!

🌾 Harvest ---

Both unstaked --- and your earnings will build up as others continue to use the contract - to get your --- back into your wallet, you will need to harvest it first!

💎 Transfer Shares

Transferring B4NK shares between addresses incurs no fees - so it's a great way to facilitate transfer of value (or paying your friends back!).
